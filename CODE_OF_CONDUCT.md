# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project aims to be welcoming and respectful for everyone.

## Expected Behaviour

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
3. Give clear, actionable feedback.
4. Be professional in issues, PRs, and discussions.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

1. Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
2. Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruptive behaviour.
3. Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behaviour that violates this code.

## Scope

This applies across repository spaces (issues, PRs, comments, and discussions).
